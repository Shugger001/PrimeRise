Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about Prime Rise botanical beverages.

What is Prime Rise?

Prime Rise creates premium botanical beverages crafted from real, functional ingredients. Each blend is designed to support hydration, balance, and everyday energy—without artificial additives.

Are your drinks natural?

Yes. Prime Rise beverages are made using natural ingredients, including fruits, herbs, and botanicals. We do not use artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. Each bottle clearly lists all ingredients for full transparency.

Where can I buy Prime Rise?

Prime Rise is currently preparing for launch. Availability will begin online, followed by select retail locations. You can join our email list for updates and early access.

How should I store the bottles?

For best quality, keep refrigerated. Shake gently before drinking, as natural ingredients may settle. Once opened, consume within 2–3 days.

Do these drinks contain caffeine?

Some blends may contain naturally occurring caffeine depending on the ingredients used (such as certain botanicals or teas). Please refer to the label on each bottle for specific details.

How are Prime Rise beverages sweetened?

Our beverages are lightly sweetened using natural sources such as fruits, honey, or dates. We do not use refined sugars or artificial sweeteners. Sweetness levels vary by blend.

Do your drinks contain preservatives?

No. Prime Rise beverages are made without artificial preservatives. Because of this, they are best enjoyed fresh and kept refrigerated.

Are your bottles recyclable?

Yes. We use glass bottles, which are fully recyclable and chosen to preserve quality while supporting a more sustainable packaging approach.

Who do I contact for partnerships or wholesale?

For wholesale, retail, or partnership inquiries, please contact us through the website or email us directly at info@promerisedrinks.com. We’re always open to collaborating with aligned partners.
